#### How to cope with gambling

Before you gamble, first consider the consequences. Before gambling, consider what you should bet on. You shouldn't bet on a lot of money. Speak to people that will give you support ex. family and friends. Do something else like other interests, hobbies. Focus on the stuff that's important for life. If you have an addiction, speak to a counselor, a therapist or a doctor.
